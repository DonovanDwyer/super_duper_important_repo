The second single from her fourth studio album.
This song was originally offered to Kylie Minogue for her ninth studio album "Body Language" (2003), but she turned it down.
With "Toxic", Britney Spears received her first and only Grammy Award for Best Dance Recording at the 2005 ceremony.
The music video for the song features references to films "Blade Runner" (1982) and "The Seven Year Itch" (1955). It is Britney Spears's most expensive music video to date, at a cost of $1 million.
The music video for "Womanizer" (2008) was created by Britney Spears as a sequel to "Toxic".
